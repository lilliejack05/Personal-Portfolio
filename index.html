About Me

I was born in Crawford, Mississippi. I grew up with my parents and still currently reside there. After graduating from high school, I quickly decided to join the United States Army serving my time on active duty, I then continued my service with the Mississippi Army National Guard.

I am currently employed with the Department of Veteran Affairs within the Medical Staff Office. Since retiring I have been looking for a new passion that will challenging enough to keep my mind engaged and lead to a new career path. I decided to enroll in a bootcamp held at Vanderbilt Univeristy & Coding Boot Camp. I am excited about this new chapter in my life and can only hope that this brings great opportunity ahead. I am excited for this opportunity.

Being a Solider for nearly half of my life, I am no stranger to the hustle and bustle of living out of a duffle-bag for either training exercises or deployments in various austere environments. Now, that I have retired I cannot wait to find a place to finally settle down and allow my roots to dig deep into the soil and grow. I am excited to start this next chapter in my life and I anticipate great opportunites that await my very near future and with the support of my family and friends I know I will be quite successful as I have thus far in life.
